From building Flash games and Dreamweaver sites as a child to selling custom MySpace pages, designing graphics and producing music throughout my teenage years, I started my first company at 18 after pitching for seed investment through a startup youth project. No degree, no bootcamp. Just 13+ years of building things, breaking things, and figuring it out since.

Over the years I've done everything from design, development and architecture to networking, security and DevOps across several industries including fintech, gaming, sports, media, streaming, e-commerce, enterprise and Web3. Improving processes has always been a passion of mine; whether it's implementing CI/CD pipelines, JIRA workflows, rebuilding legacy .NET products into modern SaaS platforms, or mentoring and running developer workshops on React, testing, design patterns and software architecture. Championing delivery, testing and output quality everywhere I've worked.

After a career break, I've been doing some consultancy work on the side to keep food in the fridge and my servers running while developing my own ideas. My desire to keep improving things, now focused on agentic engineering, has led me to what I'm building now.

<summary><b>What I'm building</b></summary>
<br>

**HibanaJS**: A lightweight, open source, NestJS-inspired backend framework for [Bun](https://bun.sh) + [Hono](https://hono.dev). Familiar decorator-based DX without the overhead. **My Flagship SaaS**: A platform I've been planning in my head for years but never had the time for. Now in active development and working towards a beta release.
